My Top 3 Movies

According to me, of course

1. 2001: A Space Odyssey

A great Sci-Fi movie about about the development of the human society, and regardless of how much it seems to change, its nature always appears to stay the same way.

2. Dune (Part 1 and 2)

Adapted from Herbert's books, the movies show how a authority figure given divine status is dangerous in its nature.

3. Shining

Another Kubrick masterpiece.
